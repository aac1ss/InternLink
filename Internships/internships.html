Social Media Content Creator Intern

Craftmysop

Offered Salary: Rs 5000 / Monthly

Location: Remote

Level: Entry

Duration: 3 Months

Job Description

We are looking for a creative and motivated intern to join our team as a Social Media Content Creator. This role involves planning, creating, and managing content for various social media platforms to help increase brand awareness and engagement.

Responsibilities

  • Develop content ideas and campaigns for social media.
  • Create and post content across multiple platforms.
  • Engage with followers and respond to comments/messages.
  • Analyze engagement metrics and adjust strategies accordingly.

Requirements

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Familiarity with social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.
  • Basic graphic design skills are a plus.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks

  • Flexible working hours.
  • Certificate of completion.
  • Opportunity to gain hands-on experience in content creation and social media management.
